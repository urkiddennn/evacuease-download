EvacuEase

EvacuEase is an AI-powered disaster preparedness platform that provides real-time evacuation assistance and site recommendations for residents of Tago. Supported by MDRRMO Tago, it offers personalized routes, alerts from MDRRMO Tago, and hazard assessments to ensure safe and efficient evacuations.
